# How to Choose the Right Custom Home Builder in the Texas Hill Country

Building a luxury custom home is one of the biggest investments you'll make. Choosing the right builder can mean the difference between a dream home and a nightmare project. Here's what we've learned after years of building in the Hill Country.

## Experience Matters

Look for a builder with a proven track record in the specific area where you want to build. The Texas Hill Country presents unique challenges — rocky terrain, flash flood zones, and strict county regulations all require specialized knowledge.

## Transparency in Pricing

A reputable builder will provide detailed cost breakdowns, not just a lump sum. Ask about their change order process, allowances for finishes, and how they handle unexpected site conditions.

## Communication Style

Your builder should be accessible and communicative throughout the process. At Koviak Built, we use BuilderTrend to give our clients real-time updates on progress, budget, and timeline.

## Quality of Subcontractors

A builder is only as good as their subcontractors. Ask about their long-term relationships with electricians, plumbers, and other trades. Consistent teams produce consistent quality.

## Check References

Don't just look at photos — talk to past clients. Ask about their experience during construction, not just the finished product. Were timelines met? Were budgets honored? Would they build again with this builder?

## Visit Active Job Sites

A well-organized job site tells you a lot about a builder's standards. Look for cleanliness, safety practices, and organized materials storage.
