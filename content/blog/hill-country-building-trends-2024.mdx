# Texas Hill Country Building Trends for 2024

The Texas Hill Country continues to attract discerning homeowners seeking luxury living in a stunning natural setting. Here are the trends we're seeing in custom home construction this year.

## Indoor-Outdoor Living

Large sliding glass walls, covered outdoor kitchens, and infinity-edge pools that blend with the Hill Country landscape are more popular than ever. Homeowners want to feel connected to the rugged beauty around them.

## Natural Stone and Local Materials

Texas limestone, reclaimed barn wood, and locally sourced stone are being used not just as accents but as primary design elements. These materials complement the landscape and age beautifully.

## Smart Home Integration

From automated lighting and climate control to whole-home audio and security systems, smart technology is becoming standard in luxury builds. We integrate these systems during construction for a clean, seamless finish.

## Energy Efficiency

High-performance windows, spray foam insulation, and solar-ready roofing are becoming baseline expectations. Hill Country homeowners want comfort and sustainability without compromising aesthetics.

## Home Offices and Flex Spaces

Remote work has permanently changed how we design homes. Dedicated offices with sound insulation, built-in technology, and separate entrances are now featured in most of our floor plans.

## Multi-Generational Living

We're designing more homes with separate living suites, kitchenettes, and private entrances to accommodate extended family. The Hill Country lifestyle is attracting families who want to be together while maintaining independence.
