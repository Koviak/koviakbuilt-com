# Why We Use BuilderTrend for Client Communication

Building a custom home is one of the largest investments you will ever make, and you deserve to know exactly what is happening with your project at every stage. That is why we use BuilderTrend -- a construction management platform that gives our clients real-time visibility into their build from groundbreaking to move-in day.

**Daily progress logs** are one of the most valued features. Every day that work occurs on your site, our project manager uploads a log with photos, notes on what was accomplished, and a summary of which trades were on site. You can view these logs from your phone, tablet, or computer at any time. No more wondering what happened while you were at work -- you can see it in photos and read the details within hours.

**The project schedule** is shared and always current. BuilderTrend displays your construction timeline as an interactive Gantt chart showing every phase, milestone, and dependency. When weather or material delays affect the schedule, adjustments are visible immediately. You always know where the project stands relative to the plan, and you can see exactly which tasks are coming up next.

**Selections tracking** eliminates the chaos of managing hundreds of decisions across dozens of categories. From tile and countertops to lighting fixtures and hardware, every selection is documented in the platform with specifications, photos, and approval status. When your tile arrives on site, our team verifies it against the approved selection in BuilderTrend before installation begins. This prevents costly mistakes.

**Financial transparency** is built in as well. You can view your budget, track change orders, and see draw schedules at any time. Every change order includes a description, cost impact, and requires your digital approval before work proceeds. There are no surprises on the final invoice because you have been tracking costs throughout the entire project.

We believe that great communication is just as important as great construction. BuilderTrend is one of the tools that helps us deliver both.
