# Understanding the Custom Home Building Process

Building a custom home can feel overwhelming if you don't know what to expect. At Koviak Built, we guide our clients through every step. Here's an overview of our process.

## Phase 1: Consultation and Planning

We start with an in-depth conversation about your vision, lifestyle needs, and budget. This includes discussing lot selection, architectural style, and must-have features.

## Phase 2: Design and Architecture

Working with our design partners, we develop floor plans and elevations that bring your vision to life. You'll review 3D renderings and make selections before construction begins.

## Phase 3: Pre-Construction

This phase includes permitting, site preparation, and finalizing the construction schedule. We provide a detailed timeline and budget breakdown so there are no surprises.

## Phase 4: Construction

The most exciting phase — watching your home take shape. Through BuilderTrend, you'll have real-time visibility into daily progress, upcoming milestones, and budget status.

## Phase 5: Finishing and Walkthrough

Interior finishes, landscaping, and final inspections come together in this phase. We conduct a thorough walkthrough with you, addressing any items before handover.

## Phase 6: Move-In and Warranty

We hand over the keys and stay available for any warranty items. Our commitment to quality doesn't end at closing.
