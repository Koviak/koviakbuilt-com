# Top 5 Features Buyers Want in a Hill Country Home

After building custom homes in the Texas Hill Country for years, we have a clear picture of what buyers value most. Whether you are building your forever home or an investment property, these five features consistently top the wish list.

**1. Panoramic Views and Walls of Glass.** The Hill Country's rolling terrain is its greatest asset, and buyers want to experience it from inside the house. Floor-to-ceiling windows, oversized sliding glass doors, and open floor plans that draw the eye outward are non-negotiable for most clients. The key is balancing glass with energy performance -- high-quality low-E coatings and proper overhangs let you enjoy the view without fighting your electric bill.

**2. A True Outdoor Kitchen.** This is not just a trend -- it is a lifestyle. Buyers want a fully equipped outdoor cooking and entertaining area with a smoker, built-in grill, refrigerator, and bar seating. Natural stone countertops and a covered structure with fans make the space usable nearly year-round.

**3. A Luxurious Master Suite.** The master retreat has become one of the most important rooms in the house. Buyers want generous square footage, a spa-quality bathroom with a freestanding tub, oversized walk-in shower, dual vanities, and a walk-in closet designed with custom built-ins. Many clients also request a private patio or balcony accessible from the master.

**4. An Oversized Garage with Workshop Space.** Hill Country homeowners tend to have hobbies -- hunting, woodworking, off-roading, or simply maintaining a beautiful property. A three- or four-car garage with additional workshop space, dedicated electrical circuits, and climate control is a highly requested feature.

**5. Energy Efficiency and Low Maintenance.** Buyers are increasingly informed about building science. Spray foam insulation, high-performance windows, tankless water heaters, and durable exterior materials like natural stone and metal roofing are expected in the luxury segment. Homes that perform well and require minimal upkeep command premium resale values.
